About Me

Path to Software Developer

My journey began when I worked as an Autonomous Vehicle Operator at a tech startup in Fremont, CA. They needed help setting up the environment for their new hardware (laptops, desktops, etc.) and I volunteered one day to help. It was honestly very basic stuff like setting up Linux and running a few lines of code provided ahead of time but the feeling that I could manipulate the computer to do anything I could imagine got me hooked.

I became curious about what this magical realm of software engineering entailed and began self-studying Ruby along with HTML and CSS basics. After spending a few months practicing and feeling relatively confident in my ability to understand the language and markup I decided to take it to the next level and go full-time learning software. I enrolled in App Academy, one of the leading bootcamps in the Bay Area with a 5% acceptance rate to their fullstack immersive program. I was very fortunate to be surrounded by hard working individuals who aided in my understanding of new languages and frameworks like Javascript, React, Redux, Ruby on Rails, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, and jQuery. I've also had the amazing opportunity to create my first full stack web application inspired by Discord (Badcord).

My recent experiences have brought me where I am today and I am excited to continue my journey forward expanding my knowledge in development and contributing to user experiences all across the web.
